Blast Media

We sell a range of blasting media for application in rust and stock removal as well as making a surface, smoother, rougher or etched.

Aluminium Oxide is the best material for sharp cutting and will cut the hardest surfaces, it is a long lasting reusable abrasive that we sell in a range of qualities going from Brown to White to Pink which is especially useful in wet blasting.

Coated Abrasives, Belts and Discs

Coated abrasives are abrasive material bonded to a flexible substrate using adhesives. This includes cloth, paper, vulcanized rubber and plastic films.

We sell a variety of cloth belts and paper belts, resin fibre discs, paper rolls and cloth rolls, waterproof wet and dry sheets, velcro discs, flap wheels, flap discs, spirabands and cartridge rolls.

Our bonded products are made with high quality materials and are used in a variety of applications on wood, metal, stainless steel polishing and finishing.

Compounds

Our compounds are used for polishing and dressing, we have a variety of sizes and compositions for different applications.

We offer hyfin, steelcut, steelbrite, lustre, albo grease, carbrax, ultrapol, rouge, polishing soap, polarshine liquid compound.

These polishing compounds are for stainless steel, aluminium, cast iron, bronze, automotive and many more applications.

Diamond Cutting Products

We sell a selection of diamond cutting tools that are coated in synthetic diamond to cut the hardest materials.

Our specialist diamond tool selection includes, dressing tools, micron powders and paste, mounting points, cutting blades and core drills.

Grinding Wheels & Cutting Discs

High performance, high quality cutting discs and grinding wheels that can be used for a variety of applications and industries.

We offer a range of grinding wheels for stainless steel, aluminium, mild steel, rubber, and other uses.

Our cutting discs are high quality and comply to safety standards and regulations.

Non-Woven

We sell scotchbrite & non woven materials that are used for scouring finished metal work, they tend to be less aggressive than coated abrasives and come in different sizes and shapes for use on portable tools to handle the most delicate and intricate jobs.

This includes unitized wheels, interleaved flap wheels, brushes, spindle mounted points, scotchbrite handpads, scotchbrite rolls, and scotchbrite belts

Polishing Mops

Used for the final finishing process of both metals and wood, smaller products can be used in intricate and precise work.

This includes WDR mops, closed stitch mops, open stitch mops, calico mops, sisal mops and other felt wheels, cones and bobs for specific purposes.

Safety Equipment

We have a range of safety equipment to suit all needs including latex gloves, nitrile gloves, rigger gloves, TIG and MIG gloves, welding gauntlets, chemsplash overalls and more.

Tungsten Carbide Burrs

Our burrs are idea for hand held operations and come in a variety of length and shaft sizes to enable easy access to all hard to reach places.

We have a full range of shapes and sizes and our burrs and tungsten carbide for lng life and durability.
