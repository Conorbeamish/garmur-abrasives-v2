Who we are

We are one of the leading abrasive product wholesalers based in Gloucestershire and offer quality products and advice to a range of industries including the automotive, engineering, woodworking, metalworking, metal fabricating and many more.

Garmur Future Abrasives has been a family run business, founded in 1973 by Ronald Beamish, we have continued to develop and expand our ability to procure and distribute abrasives supplies throughout the south west and across the country.

With over forty-five years in the industry we have extensive experience and knowledge in the abrasive industry we can offer advice and support to assist you in sourcing the best product to suit all of your requirements.​

What we do

We stock an comprehensive variety of products to further reduce lead times where possible.

If your items are known to have long lead times we can offer the option of holding stocks for you or consignment stocks should you so wish.

If you're uncertain and want to see the product in use, we can offer trials for new products where possible.

Not only this we can offer technical advice and solutions to suit your individual needs in an ever expanding and developing industry. Working with well established major manufacturers we only offer quality products to our customers.

We work closely with our customers to understand their production. We care about getting it right and ensure we supply you with the best quality products at the best price. We aim to reduce your costs and improve your finishing process.
